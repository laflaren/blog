Mornings-- you can't live with 'em, you can't live without 'em! They can be a wonderful tool to setting a standard for the rest of the day. Here are a few things I have found to be useful and realistic during Covid Times to help with some of the uncertainties and difficulties. Each is meant to be tweaked as the needs of each day arise.

  4. Rehydrate with plain ol‚Äô water and take morning supplements or medications.
